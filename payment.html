Add a payment method to your Apple ID

If you want to keep more than one payment method on file, add the payment method in Settings on iPhone or iPad, in the App Store on Mac, or in iTunes on your PC. When you edit payment information online at appleid.apple.com, additional payment methods might be removed from your account automatically.
1.Go to appleid.apple.com.
2.Tap or click Sign In, then sign in with your Apple ID.
3.Tap or click Payment Methods.
4.Tap or click Manage payment method.
5.Enter your payment information, then tap or click Update.
